Welcome to Odenaansan Dakonaan

Combining the Anishinaabe terms Odenaansan (“village or little place where my heart is”) and Dakonaan (“to hold for others), our goal is restoration of Minobimaadzwin (the good life that existed before colonization) through restoration of Anishinaabe teachings & principles.

SHAREPower

S.H.A.R.E. Power is an innovative asset management platform aimed at supporting communities in their transition to zero greenhouse gas (GHG) energy systems. The platform enables communities to manage and maintain sustainable energy solutions while fostering local economic growth. By being community-owned and operated, S.H.A.R.E. Power not only helps reduce environmental impact but also strengthens local economies by creating opportunities that benefit grassroots communities. WaterPlus

The WaterPlus initiative is dedicated to providing sustainable, clean water to Agereger Village in Eastern Uganda, where over 3,000 people currently rely on unsafe water sources. Currently working towards building a solar-powered well for the community, this ongoing effort will improve health, support local agriculture and development and, most importantly, relieve the massive burden currently on the women of the village. In partnership with local leaders, Odenaansan Uganda is committed to creating lasting, culturally appropriate infrastructure.
