First WaterPlus Project Funded!

Date: October 5th, 2024

We are thrilled to announce that as of October 5th, 2024, our WaterPlus GoFundMe campaign has successfully reached its goal of $2,500 CAD!

Click here to read the full report of the WaterPlus Agereger trip.

WaterPlus Documentary

Directed by: Victoria Hope

The WaterPlus project aims to address critical water scarcity issues impacting the community’s health, agriculture, and overall quality of life. This funding enabled our WaterPlus team (I.V. Hope, Frank Semata, John Odolon, and a camera and interview crew) to visit Agereger Village, Uganda to kick off this vital project.

During the visit, clan members expressed strong enthusiasm for the project, affirming their support for John Odolon and the Fidelitas organization to lead the effort. Elders highlighted the community's readiness to collaborate, emphasizing the project’s potential to enhance their livelihoods.

The next steps for WaterPlus involve reviewing and reaffirming the existing Memorandum of Understanding (MOU) between Fidelitas and Odenaansan Uganda. Additionally, an implementation plan for Water Plus will be developed to outline the next phase of action.

Thank you to everyone who contributed to this vital cause! If you’d like to learn more or support the WaterPlus project, please reach out to us directly. Together, we can make a significant impact in Agereger Village.
