First WaterPlus Project Funded!

Date: October 5th, 2024

Watch our documentary to learn about the success of WaterPlus's first project. The WaterPlus project aims to address critical water scarcity issues impacting the community’s health, agriculture, and overall quality of life. The success of our GoFundMe campaign enabled our WaterPlus team to visit Agereger Village, Uganda to kick off this vital project.
